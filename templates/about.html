Imaginarium

"Imaginarium" is a captivating web application that blends artificial intelligence with creative storytelling to transform simple images into immersive narrative experiences. At its core, the concept of Imaginarium revolves around using state-of-the-art machine learning models to interpret visual content and generate rich, engaging stories that are specific to the themes or moods depicted in the images.

Milestone 1: Initial Development and AI Integration

Start your journey into AI-driven storytelling with Milestone 1. This phase focuses on setting up the foundational technology stack and integrating advanced AI models to transform images into captivating narratives.

  • Image-to-Text: Utilize Hugging Faceâ€™s model to extract descriptive captions from uploaded images.
  • Story Generation: Employ OpenAI's GPT-4 to craft engaging short stories based on the captions.
  • Interactive Display: Stories are dynamically presented on a P5.js canvas with accompanying narration via the P5.js speech library.
  • Framework: Built using Flask and styled with Bootstrap for a robust and responsive user experience.

Milestone 2: Advanced Features and Enhanced Interactivity

Elevate the narrative experience in Milestone 2 by incorporating the LangChain library to add depth and interactivity to your stories. Select from various genres and enjoy contextually enriched narratives.

  • Genre Selection: Users can choose from six genres, tailoring the storytelling to their preferences.
  • Prompt Templates and RAG: Enhance the narrative quality with structured prompts and contextually rich content using Retrieval-Augmented Generation.
  • Customized Narration: Different voices from the P5.js speech library match the selected genres, enriching the auditory experience.
  • Mood-Driven Backgrounds: Background imagery on the P5.js canvas adapts to the genre, setting the appropriate mood and atmosphere.
