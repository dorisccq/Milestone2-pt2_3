Influencer

"Influencer" is an innovative web application designed to elevate social media content creation by integrating advanced AI capabilities. Beyond simple image analysis, this platform generates tailored post captions, recommends music that complements the mood or theme of your visuals, and even crafts custom lyrics. Whether you are looking for the perfect words to accompany a photo or a song to set the scene, this tool is designed to make your posts more engaging and personalized.

Milestone 1: Post Generation and AI Integration

Start your journey into AI-driven social media content creation in Milestone 1. This phase centers on establishing the core technology stack and integrating sophisticated AI models to generate engaging post content from images, laying the groundwork for a personalized and creative posting experience.

  • Image-to-Text: Utilize Hugging Faceâ€™s model to extract descriptive captions from uploaded images.
  • Story Generation: Employ OpenAI's GPT-4 to craft engaging short stories based on the captions.
  • Interactive Display: Posts are presented on a P5.js canvas with accompanying narration via the P5.js speech library.
  • Framework: Built using Flask and styled with Bootstrap for a robust and responsive user experience.

Milestone 2: Artist Options and Enhanced Interactivity

By incorporating the LangChain library including different artists' lyrics, more unique and accurate song recommendations are generated. Enhance your experience in Milestone 2 by incorporating personalized music recommendations that provide easy access to albums for seamless searching. Additionally, enjoy AI-generated lyrics crafted in the style of popular artists, adding a unique, context-rich touch to each song suggestion.

  • Genre Selection: Users can choose from six genres, choose different artists according to their preferences.
  • Prompt Templates and RAG: Enhance the narrative quality with structured prompts and contextually rich content using Retrieval-Augmented Generation.
  • Customized Narration: Different voices from the P5.js speech library match the selected genres, enriching the auditory experience.
  • Mood-Driven Backgrounds: Background imagery on the P5.js canvas are the photos of artist, offering more immersive experiences.
